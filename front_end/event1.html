Get Out the Button to Vote!

If you’re ages 13-18 and you’re interesting in learning more about voting, join us on Thursday, Sept 30 2021, to design buttons. Teens will learn about the upcoming voting cycle and design buttons revolving around civics - which can feature anything from their favorite social justice quote to their civil rights hero.

Location: NYC Public Library

Time: 1pm. NYC Public Library, Thursday, Sept 30, 2021.

Available Spots: 30
